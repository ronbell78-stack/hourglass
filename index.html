What Hourglass Is

Hourglass is not motivation, therapy, or advice. It is a decision operating system designed for high-stakes environments where emotion, pressure, and incomplete information distort judgment.

It exists to slow reaction, compress chaos into clarity, and prevent irreversible strategic error.

Who It Is For

Hourglass is built for operators — people responsible for outcomes that compound over time.

Legal conflict. Business decisions. Reputation risk. Situations where a single mistake cannot be undone.
